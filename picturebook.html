Picture Book

Photography

Though I am no professional in photography, I love taking pictures during my travels. For me, a picture can recall memories that have been forgotten better than words on a page. When sharing experiences with others, pictures have a way of sharing that experience in greater detail. I take a great deal of pictures on every trip I go on by myself, with my wife, or with family. For this reason, I hope to dedicate one of my pages to display some of my best pictures from every trip. In my opinion, there is no better way to share who I am and what I enjoy doing.
